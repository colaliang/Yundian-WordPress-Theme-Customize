								<p><strong>DISC Ultra-thin Downlight</strong><br>
No More Ceiling Space Limits<br>
DISC is an ultra-thin downlight designed to solve installation challenges caused by limited ceiling space.<br>
With an ultra-thin profile of only 13 mm, it fits easily into different ceiling types and provides greater installation flexibility. Its patented power supply ensures stable quality while supporting fast and easy installation. With an efficacy of 100 lm/W, DISC delivers bright, energy-efficient lighting with low lumen depreciation for reliable long-term performance.<br>
Available in both single CCT and 3-in-1 CCT versions, DISC can meet different application needs with greater flexibility.<br>
Equipped with premium LED chips from WELLMAX, DISC ensures stable quality and dependable performance.</p>
